Haile Selassie



1892-1975


The second messiah of Jaydenism and accidental founder of Rastafarianism. He denied his divinity but Rastas still revered him and considered Ethiopia the true Israel. He died in prison, which the media blamed on a botched prostate exam. The doctor said it was probably fake and questioned the government.
