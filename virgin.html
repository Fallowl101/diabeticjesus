Virginity Restoration Ritual


1) First, the sex-haver must pray to Bakhūlá'hā 69 times. They must be careful to not pray to Bahūlá'hā. Summoning Bahūlá'hā once will cause the ritual to fail and the person to be a permanent sex-haver.
2) Then the sex-haver must retrieve a receipt of sex from the other sex-haver to supply to a accountant. The accountant will then pray to Bahūlá'müní 3 times to ensure that the reciepts are in order.
3) Once it has been approved, a union worker must pray to Bahūlá'ūní'on once to ensure that no workers' rights were violated during the intercourse.
4) After human rights have been protected, a lawyer must pray to Bahūlá'lá twice to ensure that the sex-haver has legal protection from the person they had sex with.
5) The sex-haver must pray to Bahūlá'virgö to restore virginity.
6) Then the God Emperor must pray to Bahūlá'pápá'i to get religious sanction for the repention of sins.
7) Finally the sex haver must say "yoyoyo [REDACTED] please forgive me yo" to end the ritual
